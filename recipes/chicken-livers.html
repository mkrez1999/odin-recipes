Chicken Livers

Description

Bring a little southern comfort to your home tonight with this classic fried chicken liver recipe!

  • chicken livers
  • flour
  • corn meal
  • spices
  1. oil skillet, mix flour and spices together
  2. turn heat to medium-high and bread livers
  3. fry livers, add extra seasoning to taste. turn when bottom breading takes on a golden brown color. while livers cook, bread more for next pan or if done, prepare sides.
  4. take livers out and put on paper towel to drain grease. repeat until done. and MAKE SURE YOU TURN OFF STOVE.
  5. Enjoy!
