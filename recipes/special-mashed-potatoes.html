Special Mashed Potatoes

Description

boil em, mash em, boil em, mash em, stick em in a stew!

Ingredients

  • mashed potatoes
  • butter
  • cream
  • spices

Steps

  1. set water to boil and pour mashed potato flakes into medium to large bowl
  2. pour hot water over flakes and stir. add butter and cream to desired taste/consistency
  3. finish with spices
